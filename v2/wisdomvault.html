WisdomVault

A local desktop app for recording answers before they evaporate.

WisdomVault is a privacy-first desktop app for recording and organizing audio answers to structured prompts. Everything stays on your machine: no cloud account roulette, no sync drama, no nonsense.

← Back to Projects

What it does

  • 100% local: Recordings and data stay on your computer.
  • Project organization: Keep separate question sets for separate life domains.
  • Built-in recording: Capture audio answers directly in the app (WebM format).
  • Question rotation: Go in order or randomize, with optional emphasis on underused prompts.
  • Question controls: Edit unanswered prompts and lock answered ones.
  • Clean files: Human-readable folders organized by project and question number.

Why this exists

I wanted a simple way to preserve voice reflections over time without handing personal data to a hosted platform. This app keeps the routine straightforward: open a project, answer a question, move on.

Over time, you get an archive of your own thinking in your own voice.
