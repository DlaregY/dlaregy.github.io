Project Profile

WisdomVault

A local-first desktop app for audio journaling and project-based interview questions.

WisdomVault is a simple, privacy-focused desktop application that helps you record and organize audio answers to structured questions. All data is stored locally on your computer: no cloud, no accounts, no syncing.

← Back to Projects

Features

  • 100% Local: All recordings and data are stored on your computer.
  • Project-Based Organization: Create multiple question sets as projects.
  • Audio Recording: Built-in recording with WebM format.
  • Smart Question Serving: Use sequential or random order, with optional prioritization of least-answered questions.
  • Question Management: Edit unanswered questions and lock answered ones.
  • Clean Folder Structure: Human-browsable files organized by project and question number.

Why this exists

WisdomVault is designed for private reflection and structured storytelling. It keeps the workflow simple: open a project, answer a question, and build a durable archive of your own voice over time.

The focus is long-term usability and ownership. Because everything is local and organized cleanly, your recordings remain accessible without depending on a hosted service.
